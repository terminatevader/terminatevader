My Experience At Legoland Florida

TermiNateVader

Hello everyone I am going to talk about my experience at Legoland Florida. I first discovered the existence of Legoland from one of the commercials on The Lego Movie DVD. The commercial made Legoland look fun, so of course I wanted to go. I decided I wanted to go to the one in Florida because it is closer and it is bigger than the one in California, so in 2017 we hopped on a plane and went to Legoland Florida. Once we got to Legoland Florida I was a little disappointed, but that's probably because I was expecting some magical Lego fairy tale land or something like that. The rides were not that exciting, very mild. I am not saying the place was a dump. I am just saying it did not live up to expectations. Now let's talk about the hotel which was pretty new when we went. The hotel was actually one of my favorite hotels. The rooms had cool designs, there was a giant lego pit in the lobby, and the elevator rides were fun. So that was my experience at Legoland Florida.
