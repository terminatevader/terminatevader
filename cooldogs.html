Cool Dogs

cool bob

The first dog of the day is Barney. He is a german shepard mixed with chow and he is eleven years old. His birthday is on November 9th, and he is cooler than you by a long shot. He likes swimming and eating. When eating he will sit in front of you staring at you until you give in and feed him. He is cool, unlike you, the person reading this. He pulls off sunglasses, shirts, and even dresses (even though he isn’t a girl). You wish you were him. The second dog of the day is Hank. He is a frenchie and he has just turned one year old this month. He is an ankle biter and literally a demon. If you wear slides he will full-on bite your slides and drag you until they come off. He also likes to pick up something he isn’t supposed to have and run around until you catch him. He is also cooler than you, but not by a long shot. He likes to look out the window and bark at the poor people walking across the street. He also travels and sleeps a whole lot.
